How to create your list of ingredients

If you notify using exact concentrations or concentration ranges, you need to create a document which lists the cosmetic product’s ingredients:

  • for every new notification
  • where a product’s formulation has changed significantly

Ingredients document

Use the International Nomenclature of Cosmetic Ingredients (INCI) name and its exact concentration as a percentage weight for weight (% w/w).

Harmful ingredients

{% include "../includes/harmful_ingredients.html" %}

After you’ve added ingredients of concern, list all other ingredients in decreasing order of concentration.

Exact concentration

List all the ingredients as exact quantities using % w/w.

Concentration ranges

You must report (using % w/w) for the following ranges:

  • up to and including 0.1%
  • above 0.1%, up to and including 1%
  • above 1%, up to and including 5%
  • above 5%, up to and including 10%
  • above 10%, up to and including 25%
  • above 25%, up to and including 50%
  • above 50%, up to and including 75%
  • above 75%, up to and including 100%
