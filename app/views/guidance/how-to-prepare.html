How to prepare a cosmetic product notification

To submit a product notification you can either use data from an EU notification or give the information manually. By using the data from your EU notification you need to answer fewer questions.

If you placed products on the market:

  • before Brexit, you can upload an EU notification file, or notify manually
  • after Brexit, in the EU and UK, you can upload an EU notification file, or notify manually
  • after Brexit, in the UK but not the EU, you must notify manually

How to notify by uploading an EU notification file

  • export your data as a ZIP file for each notification – this needs to be the same format as exported from CPNP
  • upload the ZIP files to this service
  • provide additional information not contained in the EU notification file

You can upload 100 files at a time.

Products notified in the EU before Brexit

General product information

Upload

If you are using an EU notification file you do not need to provide any additional information.

Manual notification

If you are notifiying manually you need to provide:

  • product name
  • shades of the product

If the product is a kit, you must also include:

  • the name of each component in the kit
  • if they are mixed or used sequentially
  • the pH range once mixed
  • the shades of the component

Product formulation

Upload

If the formulation information for the original EU notification was a seperate document you need to add this seperatly to the UK notification. This is a seperate document to the EU notification file, we’ll ask you for it once you have uploaded the EU notification file.

Image of the product

You do not need to provide any images of the product.

Nanomaterials

Upload and manual

You only need to provide information about nanomaterials in the product if it is not listed in EC regulation 1223/2009, Annex 4, 5, or 6 or not used in accordance with the restrictions for that material.

To determine if you need to provide more information about the nanomaterial you must provide its:

  • INCI name
  • purpose

There is a seperate process for notifying nanomaterials not listed in EC regulation 1223/2009, Annex 4, 5, or 6.

Harmful ingredients

You do not need to include harmful ingredient information.

Products not notified in the EU before Brexit

General product information

Upload

If you are using an EU notification file you do not need to provide any additional information.

Manual notification

You need to provide:

  • if the product is intended for children under 3
  • the physical form of the product
  • if the product comes in an applicator
  • if the product contains Category 1A or 1B mutagens, carcinogens or reproductive toxicants (CMRs)

If the product is a kit, you must also include:

  • the name of each component in the kit
  • if they are mixed or used sequentially
  • the pH range once mixed
  • the shades of the component
