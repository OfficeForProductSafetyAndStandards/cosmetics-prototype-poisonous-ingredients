How to prepare images for notification

As part of your product notification upload the original label artwork â€“ the artwork used when the product was first placed on the market.

To help National Poison Information Services identify the product upload a photo of the product and its packaging.

You can upload up to 10 images. Files can be up to 30MB:

  • JPG
  • PDF
  • PNG

The image must be in focus and all packaging text legible.

What the image shows differs depending on the type of cosmetic product and how its packaged.

Example 1

The product has no secondary packaging (for example, a standalone bottle of shampoo).

You must supply the label artwork and you can optionally supply a clear photo of the bottle.

Example 2

The product has secondary packaging (for example, a bottle of shampoo in a box).

You must supply the label artwork and a clear photo of the box. You can optionally attach the artwork for the bottle and a photo of the bottle.

Example 3

The product contains several components (for example, hair dye).

You must supply the artwork and, a photo of the set and you can optionally attach the original labelling of each bottle and the photograph of each bottle.

If the product features mandatory information on a separate leaflet, label, tag or card, you must attach this artwork as well.
